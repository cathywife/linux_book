先查看pid路径,一般默认 /run/nginx.pid.如果没有配置pid文件路径,则在logs目录下.
<pre><code>vim  /etc/nginx/nginx.conf</code></pre><br/>

<table>
    <tr>
        <td>说明</td>
        <td>命令</td>

        <td>查看配置是否正确</td>
        <td>nginx -t</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>指定配置文件启动</td>
        <td>nginx -c xxx/nginx.conf </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>重启</td>
        <td>nginx -s reload </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>平滑重启</td>
        <td>kill -HUP /run/nginx.pid</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>快速关闭</td>
        <td>nginx -s stop</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
        <td>完整有序关闭</td>
